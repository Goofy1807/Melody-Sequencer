On the Subject of Melody Sequencer

Harmony and Melody... creating both are the musicians greatest feat.

Melody Sequencer is a module where you have to listen to different parts of a melody and arrange them accordingly. You also need to create your own parts.

4 parts is given, 4 parts need to be created.

Selecting a part

  • Select the part you want to listen to with the up and down arrows.

Listening to a part

  • Press the green button to listen to that part.
  • The notes that this part contains are displayed in the upper screen

Moving a part

  • If you need to move a part to another position, press the yellow button while you are at the part you want to move. The screen also tells you that you selected that part.
  • Move to the part you want your selected part to move to with the up and down arrows and press the yellow button again.
  • If the moved part is in the incorrect position you will incure a strike!

Creating a part

  • Select a part you want to create and press the red button. The upper screen will display "Record"
  • Input the correct sequence of notes for that part with the 24 black and white buttons
  • Pressing an incorrect note will incur a strike!
  • If your input is correct, that part will be saved

The module is solved one all parts are created and in the correct position
